Pipeline Steps Applied:

  • Step 1: MediaPipe face detection (close-up photo, used center-crop fallback)
  • Step 2: CLAHE brightness normalization + bilateral smoothing
  • Step 3: BiSeNet 19-class face segmentation (19.6% face mask coverage)
  • Step 4: AnimeGANv2 cartoon stylization (face_paint_512_v2 model)
  • Step 5: White circle inpainting (Telea algorithm)
  • Step 6: Poisson blending (cv2.seamlessClone) into template
  • Step 7: Name "Shan" rendered with white outline
